# Participant Forms

Five forms run through the GEIDA Foundation training. Together they calibrate
the training to you, capture the use case you'll work on, and turn your own
project into the case study you present on Day 4. Each form opens at a specific
point and feeds a specific module — this page explains what each one is, when it
opens, and what it's used for.

!!! note "Where to find the forms"

## 1. Training Registration & Needs Assessment Form

**When:** Completed before Day 1.
**Purpose:** Captures your role and any prior GIS/EO exposure so facilitators can
calibrate the pace and depth of Module 1. No experience is expected — this is a
Foundation course open to all operations staff; the form simply helps pitch the
introduction at the right level.
**Feeds:** Module 1 (Introduction to EO, Geoinformatics & the eToolkit).

## 2. Use-Case Selection Form

**When:** Opens during the Day 2 indicator-mapping workshop (Module 2); finalized
during Day 3 requirement mapping (Module 4).
**Purpose:** The moment you begin identifying which use case and which project-
cycle stage are most relevant to your own work. You register an initial choice
after Module 2, test it against the design exercise in Module 3, and lock it in
during Module 4's requirement-mapping activity.
**Feeds:** Modules 2 and 3; finalized in Module 4.

[NEEDS INPUT: final field list. Draft fields: selected sector; project-cycle stage
of interest (identification / design / supervision / completion); candidate use
case in your own words; the EO/GIS applications you think are relevant; a real or
pipeline IsDB project it maps to (optional at this stage).]

## 3. Minimum Project Data Submission Form

**When:** Introduced during Day 3 (Module 4) project-onboarding walkthrough.
**Purpose:** Captures the basic details of an actual or pipeline IsDB project so it
can be onboarded onto the GEIDA platform. The project you submit here becomes your
working case study on Day 4.
**Feeds:** Module 5 (both case studies draw on this data).

[NEEDS INPUT: final field list. Draft fields: project name; country; location

GEIDA platform team.]

**When:** Introduced in the Day 4 wrap-up session.
**Purpose:** Collects your feedback on the training — content, delivery, and the
platform — to improve future batches and inform GEIDA adoption.
